Kerala Houseboat Alleppey

The Kerala Houseboat experience in Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is a journey through the serene backwaters of Kerala, one of the most picturesque and tranquil destinations in India. These houseboats, traditionally called "Kettuvallams," are crafted from natural materials like bamboo, wood, and coconut fibers. They were originally used for transporting rice and spices, but now they offer a luxurious staycation experience for tourists.

Royal Palace HouseBoat

The Royal Palace Houseboat in Alleppey, Kerala, is one of the premium options available for experiencing the backwaters in luxury. While there isn't a specific houseboat named "Royal Palace," several houseboat operators offer luxurious options with names including "Royal," "Palace," or variations thereof. These houseboats typically offer top-notch amenities and services to ensure a comfortable and memorable stay.
